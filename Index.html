MedDiff compare medication lists

    Medication lists are compared showing matching medications, new medications, and discontinued medications. Changes in medications that match on compoud name are highlighted.

    Blue medications have an exact match between the two lists

    Yellow medications have a partial match between the two lists. Within these, deleted details are denoted by × (hover over to see what was deleted) and added details are denoted by green bold text

    Green medications are new on the main list compared to the reference list

    Red medications are on the reference list but not on the main list

Discalmer: This tool is not a substitue for clinical reasoning. Best matching and difference highlighting has been attempted but may be imperfect.
